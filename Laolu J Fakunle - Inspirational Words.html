Guarded. Grounded. Guided by Grace.

Featured Reflection

Imagine the best car without brakes, an AI-powered aircraft without a pilot, or a ship without a captain. Sounds adventurous? Maybe. But would you risk your life on them? For me, it’s a firm NO. And that’s exactly what a man without self-control is like—dangerous, directionless, and destined for disaster.

“Guard your heart with all diligence, for out of it flow the issues of life.” — Proverbs 4:23

Keep Pressing Forward

"If you are not pressing, you are definitely oppressed—because those who are pressing are challenged." — Bishop David Oyedepo

This echoes the words of Apostle Paul in Philippians 3:13–14 (KJV): “Brethren, I count not myself to have apprehended: but this one thing I do, forgetting those things which are behind, and reaching forth unto those things which are before, I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus.”

Let this be a word of encouragement to someone today: Keep pressing forward. Even when the journey is tough, the resistance is proof that you're moving. Don’t settle. Don’t retreat. The prize is ahead, not behind.
